Campus Life

The Astro!

A breaktime getaway for many kids, the astro is where children can play football, practice gymnastics, or just run around! The full name is 'the Astro Turf'.


The Library!

With countless books to pore over for all ages, the school library is packed. From fact to fiction, history to fantasy, all can be found within these shelves.


The Pool!

The pool, 50m wide and 2 metres deep, has a weather-proof roof, so children can train in all weather conditions. Standard 2.5 meter lanes have held many a contest, such as the annual swim gala.
